Nice to Meet You!

I'm Julia, a Frontend Developer who is passionate about building simple yet thoughtful experiences through my work. My favorite aspect is taking complex problems and deconstrucing them to create simple and easy to use tech.

Building and creating has always been at the core of what I do. I recieved a Bachelors in Landscape Architecture from the University of Florida where I fostered my love for the iterative process of project development and proven design patterns. After working for a consulting firm out of college, I sought the opportunity to involve myself at the forefront of innovation and continous learning in my lifelong career. I chose to self study software development and have found this journey to be incredibly satifying and amusingly rigorous.

When I'm not building things, I love to be outdoors on a run, drawing and painting in my home, or catching up with friends or family at a coffee shop. Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio!
