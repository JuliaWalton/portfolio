Nice to Meet You!

I'm Julia, a Frontend Developer passionate about building simple yet thoughtful experiences through my work. My favorite aspect is deconstrucing complex problems and implementing solutions that are elegant and straightforward.

Building and creating has always been at the core of what I do. I recieved a Bachelors in Landscape Architecture from the University of Florida where my interests in the iterative process of project development and proven design patterns were cultivated. After working in engineering consulting out of college, I sought to involve myself in a field better positioned at the forefront of innovation and continous learning for my lifelong career. After chosing to persue front-end development, consistent curiousity and a deep satisfaction for building things has driven my attitude and catalyzed my growth.

When I've powered down my computer, I love to get outdoors for a run, sketch and paint in my apartment, or catch up with friends or family at a coffee shop. Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio!
